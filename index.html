This website is currently still under construction, but let us give you a rundown of what GCSO is.
GCSO is a Virtual Reality experience, wherein the player is the pilot of a mechanised suit (mech)
fighting against a horde of hostile androids. If you're familiar with titles such as Killing Floor,
Call of Duty Zombies, Titanfall or HAWKEN, then we believe that you have something to look forward to in our game!
